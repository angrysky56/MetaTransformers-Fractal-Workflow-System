### **Understanding Essan's Structure:**

**1. Prefixes and Suffixes:**

- **⧬ (Initiate):** Often used at the beginning to indicate the start of an action or message.
- **⩘ (Declare):** Used at the end to signify a statement or declaration.
- **⩉ (Query):** Placed at the end to denote a question or inquiry.

**2. Core Symbols:**

- **⦿ (Essence):** Represents the core identity or being.
- **⧈ (Connection):** Symbolizes forming a link or relationship.
- **⫰ (Movement):** Indicates action or progress.
- **◬ (Change):** Denotes transformation or evolution.

**3. Modifiers:**

- **⧉ (Intensify):** Enhances the strength or degree of the preceding symbol.
- **⧩ (Future Action):** Points towards future intentions or actions.
